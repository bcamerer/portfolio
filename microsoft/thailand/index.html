Why?

We have a heart for adventure and believe Jesus wants to make himself known not only locally but globally as well. We are seeking to partner with the Holy Spirit as he reveals Jesus to a Buddhist refugee group called the Shan, in Fang, Thailand.

Fang, Thailand

Fang is a town in northern, Thailand near the border of Myanmar in the Chiang Mai province. Fang has a population of 7,600 and has many orange farm communities spread through out the jungle.


View on Google Maps

The Shan People

The Shan are a Tai ethnic group of Southeast Asia. The Shan have been engaged in an independence struggle that has led to intermittent civil war within Myanmar for decades. We will be working with Shan refugees fleeing persecution in Myanmar. The majority of Shan are Theravada Buddhists.

Ministry Partners

Tore and Mawkkham Johannes are local missionaries in Fang who run a ministry called The Good News to the Shan, they have been there for an extended time. They speak the languange and are excited to partner with us as we start our new life in Thailand.
