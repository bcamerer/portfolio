About Brandon




Brandon's UX journey started in 2011 when he picked up the book, “Don’t make me think” by Steve Krug. User experience has proven invaluable in his career as he seeks to work on making processes for users as efficient as possible while not losing elegant design. His UX skills have taken him from designing apps with his friends to doing major UX overhauls for Microsoft.


He seeks to build experiences not just for users but with them. He has seen through collaboration, people own what they help create. He doesn't assume what the users want, he simply asks. He has the ability to get people excited about new systems and lead them on an experience that will keep them coming back.
